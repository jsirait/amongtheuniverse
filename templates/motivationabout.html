Motivation

This project is inspired by my introductory Astronomy class and it comes about during the COVID-19 pandemic. As we all are well too aware, the pandemic has wrecked havoc not only physically, but also mentally for so many people. I struggled to keep my mind from worrying about the pandemic, until I first attended my introductory Astronomy class where the prof talked about the universe, how big and old it is, and how many galaxies are in it, also how we (everything that exists in this universe) are all connected to one another. I suddenly feel less isolated and less overwhelmed. The Earth is just a tiny little dot in the universe, and you and I are very fortunate to live right here right now, when the Earth is habitable, and in an era of peace. Compared to the universe, we do not last very long, and so why waste that short time worrying?

I come up with this website, Among the Universe, to hopefully help make other people feel less worried and less overwhelmed during this pandemic by looking at the (actual) big picture of our existence.
