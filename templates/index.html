Among the Universe

This website is made and presented to remind us of out place in the universe. The universe we live in is pretty darn big, with so many galaxies, stars, planets, and other matters in it. To give you a faint idea of how big our universe is: it would take us about 100,000 years to cross our galaxy (the Milky Way) from one edge to another, if we travel at the speed of light. That's just our galaxy -- it is estimated that the universe has two hundred billion galaxies!

Our Earth really is just a tiny little dot in the universe. Our existence is just a fraction of a second of the universe's age.

So next time when we feel overwhelmed, may we remember to look at the (actual) big picture and may we be able to take a deep breath and appreciate our existence right now in this big thing we call the universe.
